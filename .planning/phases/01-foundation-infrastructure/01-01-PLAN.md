Static factory methods: Result.Success(), Result<T>.Success(T value), Result.Failure(Error error), Result<T>.Failure(Error error). Implicit conversion from T to Result<T> for ergonomic usage. Throw InvalidOperationException if accessing Value on failure or Error on success.
