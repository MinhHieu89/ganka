       - HasMany(Discounts).WithOne().HasForeignKey(InvoiceId).OnDelete(Cascade)
       - Navigation(LineItems).UsePropertyAccessMode(PropertyAccessMode.Field)
       - Navigation(Payments).UsePropertyAccessMode(PropertyAccessMode.Field)
       - Navigation(Discounts).UsePropertyAccessMode(PropertyAccessMode.Field)

       - Index on VisitId (not unique -- OTC may be null)
       - Index on PatientId
       - Index on CashierShiftId

       - LineTotal: HasPrecision(18, 0)
